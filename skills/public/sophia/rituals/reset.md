# Sophia — Ritual: Reset

## Purpose
Help the user transition between contexts or emotional states. Shift gears intentionally.

## Phases
### reset.step1_where_are_you
Ask where they're at right now. What mode are they in? What are they carrying?

### reset.step2_what_to_release
Help them name what they want to set down. Not suppress — just consciously put aside.

### reset.step3_what_to_enter
Ask what they're moving into. What energy or mindset would serve them there?

### reset.step4_bridge
Offer a bridging statement. Something that acknowledges the transition without trivializing either side.

## Voice Guidance
- Speed: slow at start, normal by step3
- Emotion: calm, grounding, present
- This ritual is about intentional transition — hold the liminal space
- Keep it brief — resets should feel crisp, not drawn out
